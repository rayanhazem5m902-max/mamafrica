About Sudan Aid

Dedicated to serving humanity with integrity, compassion, and transparency.

A Thriving Sudan

We envision a Sudan where every individual has access to basic human rights, quality education, and sustainable livelihoods. A nation rebuilt on the pillars of hope and resilience.

Serving with Compassion

Our mission is to deliver life-saving aid and long-term development programs to the most vulnerable communities in Sudan, focusing on health, education, and emergency relief.

  • Emergency Response
  • Healthcare Infrastructure
  • Educational Support

Our Core Values

Compassion

We act with empathy and kindness.

Integrity

Transparency in all our actions.

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, Sudan Aid started as a small group of volunteers providing food aid during a crisis. Today, we operate in 15 states, impacting over 500,000 lives annually.

2010

Organization founded in Khartoum.

2015

Launched first major health campaign.

2023

Reached 500,000 beneficiaries milestone.
