About us

Our vision

To see Sudanese and African society Achieving Peace and Better Life. Free From Violence and Displacement, with Access to Basic Rights, Opportunities, and The Tools to Rebuild Their Lives and Communities.

Our value

Participation
We Believe In Working Together With Communities, Partners, And Volunteers To Create Positive And Lasting Impact.
Integrity
Acting with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all we do.
Transparency
We Are Committed To Openness By Sharing Accurate Information About Our Activities, Decisions, And Use Of Resources.
Accountability
We Take Full Responsibility For Our Actions And Ensure That We Deliver Our Services Ethically And Effectively To Those We Serve.

Our mission

Dedicating effort to mitigate poverty, hunger, illiteracy, and disease. by delivering protection, humanitarian relief, and educational opportunities, while strengthening community resilience and advocating for lasting peace and justice.

Our history

MAOHS is a Sudanese National Non-Governmental voluntary organization registered under code T 1232, dated December 2022, renewed to 20/03/2024. Founded by 36 members in North Darfur state, it established a head office in El Fasher and moved there. It has sub-offices in Kutum. It engages in partnership with Autash, Tabashir, DAID, Common Medical Association, and NRC. It is a cluster member of WASH, Nutrition, Health, Education, FSL, Child Protection, and Peacebuilding working group.
