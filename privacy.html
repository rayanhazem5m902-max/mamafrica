Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us...

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of all our beneficiaries, donors, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in connection with our humanitarian programs in Darfur.

Information Collection

We may collect the following types of personal information: Direct personal information: Name, email address, phone number, and postal address. Sensitive information: Health conditions or special needs required for aid delivery. Financial information: Payment details for donations. Usage data: Interaction with our website, including cookies and analytics.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes: Delivering humanitarian aid effectively in Darfur. Communicating with beneficiaries, donors, and partners. Preparing internal reports and updates for donors or stakeholders. Ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Data may be shared with government authorities when required by law and with trusted partners assisting in program delivery under strict confidentiality agreements. Your personal information will only be shared with your consent when necessary to fulfill our humanitarian mission.

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information, including secure storage, restricted access to staff, encryption of sensitive financial and health data, and regular monitoring and backups to prevent unauthorized access. All personal information is treated with the highest level of care to maintain confidentiality.ct.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze website performance, and improve our services. Users can manage or disable cookies through their browser settings, although some features of the site may not function properly without them.
